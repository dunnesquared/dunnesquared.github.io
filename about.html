About

As I mentioned in the home-page blurb, my name is Alex Dunne. I live in Montreal, Quebec, Canada, where I've been teaching and tutoring high school math for over seven years.

Looking for new professional challenges, I have spent the last year brushing up my programming skills as part of my return to software development. I say 'return' because I actually do have a Bachelors degree in Software Engineering. Life took me in other directions after graduating, but I never lost my interest in computer science and coding.

Anyway, for any potential employers out there, here's my stack so far. Having studied C++ and Java in university, I really took to the realtively simpler syntax and faster feedback of Python. It's the language I feel the most comfortable working with at present.
